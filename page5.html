The Apple Macbook Air

The Apple MacBook Air was released in 2006 by the commonly known company, Apple. This device usually costs around 1,099 dollars and is cheaper than the Apple MacBook pro. These laptops are like each other considering they function in similar ways. Within its design, it has a camera, a remote control, and many other modern features. There are three diverse types of this model, and they vary in their structural design. Some MacBooks offer faster software, CPU, which increases the price of the product. The MacBook can be commonly found in black and white colors and is around 13 inches in size. Some of the negative aspects of this model may include that it does not have a media card reader and it does not have an Express Card slot or FireWire 800. However, this product does have many positive attributes as well. It weighs only 5.1 pounds, and it is 9 inches deep like other modern-day laptops. It has a functional touchscreen, keyboard, and touchpad. It has a resolution of 1,280x800 which allows users to view more clearly. The Apple MacBook 2006 model has Intel GMA 950 graphics. One interesting function of the device is the AC adaptor. The AC adaptor is magnetic to where the cord will detach itself if someone were to trip on the cord. In conclusion, Apple has made many technological advancements over the years and the 2006 model of the MacBook was a remarkable success. Their company had much profit from their product in a variety of ways.
